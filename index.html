Query Spikes and Related Queries

I was the original design of the Funnelback "Pattern Analyser" system, which can detect "spikes" in particular queries (usually due to some external temporal event). I also designed and implemented a system to automatically detect related queries, based on their click patterns. This can be used to group queries which have no terms in common but have the same semantics.
